Top 10 Places to Visit in India

1. Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal is a stunning white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan for his beloved wife Mumtaz. It exemplifies perfect symmetry and is considered one of the greatest architectural achievements in the world.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹18,000 - ₹25,000

2. Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Varanasi is known for its ghats, evening Ganga Aarti, ancient temples, and the deeply spiritual atmosphere. It’s an incredible place to witness traditional Indian culture and rituals.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹12,000 - ₹20,000

3. Golden Temple, Amritsar

The Golden Temple is the spiritual and cultural center for the Sikh religion. It features a stunning golden structure surrounded by a holy lake and is known for its hospitality and langar.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹15,000 - ₹22,000

4. Kerala Backwaters

The backwaters of Kerala offer a tranquil experience through interconnected canals, rivers, and lakes fringed by palm trees. Houseboat stays are a popular and relaxing option.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹20,000 - ₹30,000

5. Leh-Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir

Leh-Ladakh is a high-altitude desert with otherworldly landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and thrilling adventure activities. It’s ideal for road trips and spiritual exploration alike.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹25,000 - ₹35,000

6. Jaipur, Rajasthan

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is filled with royal palaces, bustling bazaars, and historic forts like Amer Fort and Hawa Mahal. It is rich in heritage and culture.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹15,000 - ₹22,000

7. Goa

Goa is famous for its golden beaches, vibrant nightlife, seafood cuisine, and a unique blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures. It's perfect for both relaxation and partying.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹18,000 - ₹28,000

8. Hampi, Karnataka

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that boasts ancient ruins and magnificent temples set among dramatic landscapes. A paradise for history lovers and photographers.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹12,000 - ₹18,000

9. Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Manali is a popular hill station nestled in the Himalayas, offering breathtaking views, adventure sports like paragliding and river rafting, and access to the scenic Solang Valley and Rohtang Pass.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹16,000 - ₹24,000

10. Darjeeling, West Bengal

Darjeeling is a charming hill station known for its tea gardens, the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, and panoramic views of the Kanchenjunga range.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹16,000 - ₹24,000
