Top 10 Places to Visit Outside India

1. Paris, France

The romantic capital of the world, Paris is home to world-famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum. Take a stroll along the Seine, enjoy fresh croissants in a sidewalk café, or marvel at the art and architecture in every corner.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹1,10,000 - ₹1,50,000

2. Bali, Indonesia

Bali offers a perfect mix of culture, nature, and spirituality. You can relax on serene beaches, explore ancient temples like Tanah Lot and Uluwatu, or trek through rice terraces and volcanoes. It's budget-friendly and ideal for solo travelers, couples, and families.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹60,000 - ₹90,000

3. Dubai, UAE

Dubai dazzles with its futuristic skyline, luxurious shopping malls, and thrilling desert adventures. Visit the iconic Burj Khalifa, take a boat ride in Dubai Marina, or visit the Palm Jumeirah. It’s an extravagant yet surprisingly accessible destination.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹70,000 - ₹1,00,000

4. London, UK

A blend of royal charm and modern flair, London is famous for its red buses, Tower Bridge, Buckingham Palace, and British Museum. It’s a great city for exploring on foot or by underground, with cultural festivals and West End theatre shows year-round.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹1,20,000 - ₹1,70,000

5. Singapore

This ultra-modern city-state is clean, safe, and incredibly efficient. Attractions like Marina Bay Sands, Sentosa Island, Universal Studios, and the futuristic Gardens by the Bay make it a family favorite. The local food is also a major draw!

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹80,000 - ₹1,15,000

6. Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is where ancient traditions meet sci-fi modernity. Visit the historic Meiji Shrine, witness sumo wrestling, shop in Shibuya, or unwind in an onsen bath. Cherry blossom season in spring is especially magical.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹1,20,000 - ₹1,80,000

7. New York City, USA

The Big Apple is buzzing with energy 24/7. See the Statue of Liberty, catch a Broadway show, walk through Central Park, or explore the world-class entertainment and eateries. Every neighborhood offers something unique.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹1,50,000 - ₹2,00,000

8. Rome, Italy

A living museum, Rome is packed with iconic landmarks such as the Colosseum, Pantheon, Trevi Fountain, and Vatican City. Enjoy authentic Italian pizza and gelato as you explore cobblestone streets brimming with history.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹1,10,000 - ₹1,60,000

9. Sydney, Australia

A coastal gem, Sydney boasts the famous Opera House, Harbour Bridge, and sunny beaches like Bondi and Manly. It's a laid-back city perfect for nature lovers and city explorers alike.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹1,40,000 - ₹1,90,000

10. Cape Town, South Africa

Known for its dramatic natural beauty, Cape Town offers majestic views from Table Mountain, wine tasting in Stellenbosch, and penguin spotting at Boulders Beach. It’s great for adventure, culture, and stunning scenery.

Estimated cost for 5-day trip: ₹1,00,000 - ₹1,50,000
